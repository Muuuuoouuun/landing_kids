# Landing Page Plan: EdTech for Kids

## Section Plan (15 Sections)

1. **Hero Header**: Captivating animation/video + Main value proposition.
2. **Problem Statement**: What makes learning hard or boring for kids?
3. **Solution / Philosophy**: Our "Play-to-Learn" approach.
4. **Key Feature 1 (Core)**: Personalized learning path animation.
5. **Key Feature 2 (Gamification)**: Rewards and badges system.
6. **Key Feature 3 (Safety)**: Parent-approved safe environment.
7. **Testimonials (Parents)**: Success stories and trust indicators.
8. **Testimonials (Kids)**: Why they love the app (fun/cool).
9. **Interactive Preview**: A "Try Now" mini-game module.
10. **Curriculum Overview**: Educational standards mapping.
11. **Subscription/Pricing**: Clear, parent-friendly pricing tiers.
12. **Meet the Team / Science behind it**: Pedagogy and expertise.
13. **FAQ**: Handling parent concerns (screen time, privacy).
14. **Social Proof**: Press/awards/partnerships.
15. **Footer / CTA**: Final hook and download buttons.
